Value Propositions


WallBee develops and delivers unique web-based Transport Management Systems, specially designed by Freight Forwarders to Freight Forwarders, Courier Companies and Logistics Providers.

With interface to most major Web-shops, Express- and Postal Companies, WallBee offers you the most comprehensive Transport Management System in the Market, giving you and your clients access to multiple Carriers and services in one system only.

By focusing on the door-door segment, WallBee will give you easy and unlimited access to the fast-growing market within the Express, Postal and Distribution segment. At the same time, you avoid dilution and gain the opportunity of additional revenue from your existing customer base.

The WallBee Transport Management System is tailor made by Freight Forwarders - showing transport modes, best price practice and transit times on multiple carriers in one system only. The system is extremely user friendly and can be used both by you and your end clients.

Finally, the system is scalable giving you the opportunity to operate in multiple countries, connecting multiple branches and setup unlimited numbers of clients. This will give you full overview and transparency within this business segment. With this structure and process in place you will have far less administration, increased productivity and therefore considerable unit cost reductions.

What’s in it for you





Flexibility – access to multiple carriers and services in one system

Having access to multiple carriers and services in the same portal, will give you the opportunity to optimize your transportation cost and provide the best transport solution always i.e. delivering the right service, quality and price to your customers.

Best service/quality practice

The system provides you with a list of service alternatives for a given destination i.e. domestic, import, export and cross trade shipments. Your clients can easily choose the right service in terms of price, quality, and transit times.

100% visibility

From a management point of view the system accumulate data and information across Regions, Countries, Branches and Clients, giving you an instant overview of number of shipments, transport costs, gross profit, transport sale, performance etc. Financial Dashboard and Performance Dashboard give you instant information about your business.

Easy administration - Productivity increase

The system is designed to ease the administration in all aspects of your business. Selling Postal, Express and Distribution services is often low yield and you must automate all processes. With the build-in toolbox, the system will help you to manage all processes from A to Z, i.e. Pickup management, automated Cost controlling, invoicing of clients, Sales management etc.

Customer Service / Track & Trace

There is a build-in customer service module, which will make it easy for you to follow up on all shipments, that has been generated in the system. This way you can offer “true” and “proactive” customer service to your client. If there are any discrepancies you will see it immediately. The system is linked to all carriers and receive automatic Track &Trace information. Latest track event will always be shown in the history overview.

Automatic reconciliation of Carriers invoices and Client invoicing

The system handles cost and sales prices and can easily make an automatic reconciliation of incoming carrier invoices. At the same time, the system forms the basis for the invoicing of you end client. Invoicing data can be transferred via API to your ERP/Accounting system or we can even produce the final invoice for your clients. No invoicing mistakes – and only one invoices with multiple services presented to your client.
