About Us

WallBee develops and delivers unique web-based Transport Management Systems (TMS), specially designed for Freight Forwarders, Courier Companies and Logistics Providers.

With interface to all major Web-shops, Express- and Postal Companies, WallBee offers you the most comprehensive Transport Management System in the Market, giving you and your customers access to multiple Carriers and services in one system only.

The WallBee system offers you lowest cost routing, best price practice, rate calculation, Pickup management, automatic cost controlling, invoice specifications, track & trace, reporting, Dashboards and much more.

Features & Benefits



Flexibility – access to many carriers and services in one system

Having access to many carriers and services in the same portal, will give you the opportunity to optimize your transportation cost and provide the best transport solution always i.e. delivering the right service, quality and price to your customers.

Best service/quality practice

The system provides you with a list of service alternatives for a given destination for both import, export and cross trade shipments. You just choose the right service in terms of price, quality, and transit times.

100% visibility across branches/sites

From a management point of view the system can provide you with data and information across all your branches/sites i.e. giving you an instant overview of number of shipments, transportation spends per branch, transport sale, performance etc. All branches will be linked to an organization so you will be able to aggregate data for the whole company.

Web-based (Easy to implement at all branches/sites)

Setting up multiple branches in the system is quite easy since everything is web-based. The system is scalable and designed to grow together with your business.

Ease of use (15 min. education and you can use the system)

It takes only 15 minutes to educate a user. Combardo will offer regular webinars. Date and times will be published on our webpage.

Easy administration

The system is designed to ease the administration in all aspects of your business. Selling postal, express and distribution services means that you must automate all processes. The system will help you to manage all processes from A to Z and even provide you with the necessary information for you to invoice your customers, cost controlling, etc.

Multiple accounts per customer

The system can operate with multiple accounts per customer. This will give you the flexibility to setup a customer profile with multiple accounts, if the customer require invoice specifications per department or per site.

One history for all shipments

All shipments produced in the system will be gathered in one history. From the history you will have access to online Track & Trace information, shipment information, and statistics. There are several search options such as AWB, references, destination country, imports, exports etc.

Full Track & Trace for all services

Our system is linked to all carriers and we receive automatic Track & Trace information. Latest track event will always be presented in the history overview

Address Book with full import and export

It’s possible to either import addresses directly from your WMS, Order management systems or to create addresses manually in the system. All your addresses can be exported as well.

Reporting statistics

All your data in the system can be exported to a csv file. There will even be some standard reports available including a graphic overview on your daily, weekly or monthly performance. It’s our ambition to provide you with real time data and create visibility, which will enable you to get a better overview, to take the right decisions based on facts and to manage your business in general.

Notifications to consignees / shippers

When you create a shipment in the system, the system will automatically generate an e-mail to the consignee if it’s an export/domestic shipment. If it’s an import shipment, the system will generate an e-mail and an airway bill, which will be sent directly to the shipper.

Integration to accounting-, order -, warehouse management system

It is possible to integrate external system to the Combardo system. We offer several solutions such as API, XML, drop file, flat files etc.
